Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked pasta dish made from wide, flat sheets of pasta. The pasta is layered with fillings like cheese, meat, vegetables, tomato sauce, and white sauce

Lasagna can be eaten within two days if it's handmade. Cooked and stuffed lasagna can be kept in the fridge for 3â€“4 days. If frozen, it can keep up to one month.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
  7. Layer the meat sauce
  8. Layer the noodle
  9. Layer the ricotta mixture
  10. Layer the mozarella sauce
  11. Layer the meat sauce again
  12. Layer the parmesan cheese
  13. Repeat the layers and top with parmesan cheese

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
